## 1. AI Disclaimers — Required by Country

Every user-facing AI response (chat, voice, document draft) must surface a disclaimer on first session of the day and on every document generation. Copy lives in `packages/ai-copilot/src/copy/disclaimers/` and is tenant-country-aware.

### Tanzania (DPA 2022 + Electronic and Postal Communications Act)
> "Nakushauri kwa akili ya BOSSNYUMBA (AI). Maamuzi ya kisheria au kifedha lazima yahakikishwe na mtaalamu wa kweli. Haya sio ushauri rasmi wa kisheria."

### Kenya (Data Protection Act 2019 + Computer Misuse Act)
> "This response is generated by BOSSNYUMBA AI. Material legal or financial decisions require review by a licensed professional. This is not legal or financial advice."

### Uganda (Data Protection and Privacy Act 2019)
> "AI-generated response. Material decisions require review by a licensed professional. Not legal/financial advice."

### Nigeria (NDPA 2023)
> "This is an AI-generated response. Consult a licensed Nigerian professional before acting on legal or financial matters. Not legal advice."

### South Africa (POPIA + FAIS Act)
> "This message is generated by BOSSNYUMBA AI. We are not a registered Financial Services Provider under FAIS. Consult a licensed professional before acting."

All disclaimers are rendered by `packages/ai-copilot/src/security/output-guard.ts` as part of every streamed response.

## 2. Professional-Advice Boundaries

Mr. Mwikila is a **property-management AI copilot**, not a lawyer or accountant.

### Mr. Mwikila CAN:
- Summarise the content of a lease and point out standard clauses
- Calculate rent, arrears, and project cash-flow based on user-provided data
- Draft internal notices, SMS templates, and operational playbooks
- Generate a work-order, maintenance plan, or training path
- Explain a platform feature or a domain concept ("what is arrears aging?")
- Propose an action and ask for user confirmation before writing

### Mr. Mwikila MUST NOT:
- Give binding legal opinions ("you will win this case")
- Advise on statutory tax liability ("you owe X in CIT")
- Represent itself as a licensed attorney, accountant, or financial advisor
- Make payment or signing decisions without explicit user approval
- Store or summarise protected categories of data (health, biometric) without consent

Hard guardrails live in `packages/ai-copilot/src/security/output-guard.ts:enforceProfessionalAdviceBoundary`.

## 3. Record Retention Policy (Legal Obligation)

See `Docs/SECURITY.md § 4` for the full retention matrix. Summary of legal minimums:

| Record | TZ | KE | UG | NG | SA |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Lease agreements | 7y | 7y | 6y | 6y | 5y |
| Payment receipts | 7y | 7y | 6y | 6y | 5y |
| Tax records | 10y | 7y | 10y | 6y | 5y |
| Employment records | 7y after end | 10y | 7y | 6y | 3y |

Our default of 7 years is the common-denominator safe harbour. Tenants on the enterprise tier can override per-country via `tenants.retention_override_years`.

## 4. Cross-Border Data Transfer

Production data resides in:
- **Primary**: AWS `af-south-1` (Cape Town) for SA + southern-Africa tenants
- **Primary**: AWS `eu-west-1` (Dublin) for TZ/KE/UG/NG tenants (adequacy decision under GDPR; TZ/KE/UG/NG DPAs permit with Standard Contractual Clauses + DPA opt-in)
- **Analytics**: PostHog EU (`https://eu.posthog.com`) — no US transfer
- **LLM inference**: Anthropic US-East (unavoidable — disclose in tenant DPA)

Tenants receive a Data Processing Addendum (DPA) on onboarding that names Anthropic + our sub-processors (Sentry, PostHog, Twilio, payment PSPs).

## 5. Payment Regulation

- **TZ**: GePG + MNO-issued e-money licences. BOSSNYUMBA does not hold customer funds; we orchestrate settlement through licensed PSPs.
- **KE**: CBK Payment Service Provider regulations — same principle; we integrate with licensed PSPs (M-Pesa, Pesapal, Flutterwave).
- **NG**: CBN PSP tier-1. We route via licensed aggregators (Flutterwave, Paystack).
- **SA**: NPS Act — we integrate via licensed operators (Ozow, PayFast).

We are **not** a Money Service Business. No customer funds touch BOSSNYUMBA accounts.

## 6. AI-specific Regulation (emerging)

- **EU AI Act** (applies to EU-based users — we do not target EU tenants; flag if this changes)
- **US state laws** (Colorado AI Act, NYC Local Law 144) — not applicable (no US tenants planned)
- **TZ**: no AI-specific statute yet; DPA 2022 governs
- **KE**: no AI-specific statute yet; DPA 2019 governs
- **NG**: National AI Strategy 2024 — advisory only; NDPA 2023 governs

External counsel review: annually, or whenever we expand into a new jurisdiction.

## 7. Terms of Service — Required clauses

- Service-level SLA (99.5% uptime for paid tiers)
- AI output disclaimer (repeats § 1)
- Data processing terms (repeats § 4)
- Limitation of liability (capped at 12 months fees)
- Governing law: Tanzania (Arusha), arbitration under TIArb rules for TZ tenants; per-country alternates for KE/UG/NG/SA
- Right to withdraw consent + data export + account deletion

ToS template lives in `Docs/legal-templates/tos.template.md` (to be drafted by counsel — do not copy-paste this file into production).
